Safety Tips For Canoeists

  1. Be able to swim
  2. Wear a life jaccket at all times
  3. Do not stand up or move around. If canoe tips,
    • Hang on the canoe
    • Use the canoe for support
    • Swim to shore
  4. Don't overexert yourself
  5. Use a bow light at night
